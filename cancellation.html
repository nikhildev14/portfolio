Cancellation & Refund Policy

           bsdagct.org also offers a return policy to its Donors. It refunds its donors their money that they had donated for bsdagct.org without proper knowledge of terms and conditions and also if at all they donate the money unwillingly.


You can expect to receive the refund within 4 weeks from shipping your return receipts. The information regarding your return will be notified you via email with the details of your refund.


Notes

- The refund will be made to the same payment option(s) originally used to pay for the Donation.
- Please note that the refund will be done based on the current Rupees.
- Our Online Payment Gateway accepts payment only in Indian Rupees.
- The decision of refund shall be of trustees whether they want to refund the amount or not.

The focus of the trust is on providing access to education, healthcare, and other basic necessities to those living in rural areas. It also works to promote the principles of equality, justice, and social inclusion, as advocated by Dr. Ambedkar..
